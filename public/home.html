Problems We Do Not Offer

There are many problems that are faced by solar powered Homes. Why not make a better Livelihood. Refer the Following we do not Offer.

Affordability

High Upfront Costs Deter Many Households From Adopting Solar Energy. Many Households Fear The Costs Of Setting Up Solar Energy Due To Personal Interests, Living Need, Poverty or Locality Conditions.

Energy Reliability

Cloudy/Rainy Days and Nights Result In Inconsistent Energy Generation Which Can Rarely Seem To Be Fatal Due To Natural Events Which Causes Damage Not Only To The People Living In The Homes But Also To The External Environment.

Maintenance

Challlenges Like Dirt Accumulation, Battery Wear, and Cleaning Persist Causes Discrepancies And The Fear Of Solar Panels Being Obstructed By Natural Beings Or Natural Calamities.

Space Constraints

Many Homes Lack Adequate Space For Traditional Solar Supports. This is due to strong House/ Roof required for placing the Solar Panel which is often regretted by Rural Areas despite huge amount of Solar Insulation Received.

Social Resistance

Concerns About Aesthetics And Societal Judgement Hinder Adoption. Communities Refuse The Usage Of Solar Panelswhich often Ignites Conflicts & The Design Of Solar Panels Spoils The Aesthetic Designs Of Their Living.

Cost-Effectiveness

Many People Prefer Monthly Electricity Bills Over One Time Huge Investment Plans So As To Sustain Their Livelihood. Many Yet Prefer Hydro Electricity Because It Is The Cheapest Source Of Energy For Everyone Including The Poor and Middle-Class.

FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

What are Ecoshades solar blinds?

Ecoshades are Smart solar-powered blinds that generate electricity while providing shade, helping you save on energy costs, not only this but also provide your Home a modern, Aesthetic and an advanced technology to use at ease.

How do Ecoshades work?

They use built-in solar panels to absorb sunlight and convert it into electricity in simple processes which can be used to power devices or stored in a battery.

What makes Ecosshades different from the other?

Unlike traditional blinds, Ecoshades generate renewable energy, can be con trolled remotely via the app, and adjust/rote automatically according to the direction of sun using arduino technology.

How do I Install EcoShades?

Ecoshades can be easily installed like normal blinds. Following the installation guide that is provided in the app or contact our support team for assistance if the Instruction leaflet is not clear.

Do I need Wi-fi Connection for EcoShades to work?

Wi-fi is required for remote control and monitoring. However, bluethooth model is also available for remote areas who do not have internet provision. Basic functions like manual adjustment can work without the internet via arduino tech.

How do I Connect my Ecoshades to the App?

Open the EcoShades App. Go to Device Setup and enable Bluethooth/Wi-fi for which you want to connect the product. Select "Add Device" and follow the on-screen instructions.

Can I Integrate Ecoshades with my smart home system?

Yes, Ecoshades can be connected to Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Apple Homekit for voice control and automation sice under the same network connection or the bluetoot connection.

Can I schedule my blinds to open and close automatically?

Yes! Use the Auto mode feature in the app to set schedules based on time or sunlight levels.

How do I monitor the energy my blinds generate?

You can track real-time energy generation in the Energy dashboard section of the app.

How much energy does EcoShades produce?

Energy output varies based on sunlight exposure and panel size. On average, a 1m square area of EcoShade generates around 100-200W per Hour in Direct Sunlight.

Can I store the energy for later use?

Yes! Ecoshade can be connected to a battery storage unit to save energy for nighttime use.

Do Ecoshades require maintenace?

Minimal maintenance is need like dusting and prevention from water to react with circuits. Easier to maintain rather than solar panels which take a lot of space and cause inconvinience to maintain.

My Ecoshades are not responding? What should I do?

Try these steps: 1. Check if the device is powered and connected to your existing/utilizing Wi-fi or bluetoot. 2. Restart the EcoShades and the App. 3. Ensure firmware is up-to-date i.e updated. 4. If the issue continous, contact customer care.

What happens if there is no sunlight?

Ecoshades still function as normal blinds. If connected to a battery, stored energy can be used during low sunlight hours.

How much does EcoShades Cost?

Pricing varies based on size and features(w-fi/bluetoot),etc... Check the app or website for the latest prices.

Do Ecoshades come with a warranty?

Yes we offer 1-3 year warranty depending on the model.

Can I return or exchange my EcoShade?

We offer a 30 day return policy if the product is unused and is in its original packaging.
